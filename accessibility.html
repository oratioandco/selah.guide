Accessibility Statement

Last updated: January 2025

Our Commitment to Accessibility

Tobias Treppmann is committed to ensuring that our website https://selah.guide is accessible to all users, including those with disabilities. We strive to provide an inclusive digital experience that enables everyone to access our content and services effectively.

Accessibility Standards

We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA standards and the European Standard EN 301 549, as required by the German Barrier-Free Information Technology Ordinance (BITV 2.0).

These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with disabilities and user-friendly for everyone.

Compliance Status

This website is partially compliant with WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards. We are continuously working to improve accessibility and achieve full compliance.

Accessibility Features

We have implemented the following accessibility features:

  • Semantic HTML structure for screen readers
  • Alternative text for images
  • Keyboard navigation support
  • High contrast color schemes
  • Responsive design for different screen sizes
  • Skip links for main content
  • Proper heading hierarchy
  • ARIA labels and roles where appropriate

Known Limitations

We are aware of the following accessibility limitations and are working to address them:

  • Some interactive elements may not be fully optimized for screen readers
  • Video content may lack comprehensive audio descriptions
  • Some color contrast ratios may not meet the highest standards in all contexts

We are committed to addressing these issues in upcoming updates to our website.
