Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Overview

Selah is designed with your privacy in mind. We access only the minimum information necessary to provide mindful meeting transitions, and all data remains on your device.

Information We Access

Calendar Data

  • We access your calendar events through macOS Calendar (EventKit)
  • This includes: meeting titles, times, locations, attendees, and meeting URLs
  • Calendar data is only accessed locally on your device
  • We do not transmit calendar data to any servers

User Preferences

  • Settings like notification timing and selected calendars
  • Meeting intentions and reflection notes you create
  • All stored locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults

Information We DON'T Collect

  • No personal information is transmitted to our servers
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No third-party integrations
  • No cloud storage of your data

Local Notifications

Selah uses local notifications to remind you about upcoming meetings. These are scheduled on your device and don't involve any external services.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your Mac using standard macOS storage mechanisms:

  • UserDefaults for preferences
  • Local app container for any cached data
  • No external databases or cloud services

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any information with third parties.

Security

Your data remains under the protection of macOS security features:

  • App Sandbox ensures Selah only accesses permitted resources
  • Calendar access requires explicit user permission
  • Notification permissions are managed by macOS

Your Rights

Since all data is local to your device:

  • You can delete all app data by uninstalling Selah
  • You can revoke calendar access in System Settings
  • You can disable notifications at any time

Children's Privacy

Selah is designed for professional use and is not directed at children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
