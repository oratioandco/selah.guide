Frequently Asked Questions

Getting Started

How do I grant calendar access?

When you first launch Selah, you'll be prompted to grant calendar access. If you missed this:

  1. Open System Settings
  2. Go to Privacy & Security → Calendars
  3. Enable access for Selah
  4. Restart Selah

How do I set meeting intentions?

Click on any meeting in the dropdown menu to open the details view. You'll see a text field where you can write your intention for that meeting. For recurring meetings, your intention is saved automatically.

Troubleshooting

Selah isn't showing my meetings

Check these common issues:

  • Ensure calendar access is granted in System Settings
  • Verify your calendars are selected in Selah Settings
  • Check that your Calendar app is syncing properly

Notifications aren't appearing

To fix notification issues:

  1. Check System Settings → Notifications → Selah
  2. Ensure notifications are allowed
  3. Verify Do Not Disturb is not active (or configure DND settings in Selah)
  4. Check your notification timing in Selah Settings

Features

Can I customize the breathing pattern?

Currently, Selah uses the scientifically-backed 4-7-8 breathing pattern. We may add customization options in future updates.

Does Selah work with all meeting platforms?

Selah detects meeting links for:

  • Zoom
  • Microsoft Teams
  • Google Meet
  • Webex
  • Any URL in the location or notes field

Privacy & Data

Is my calendar data private?

Yes! All calendar data stays on your device. Selah doesn't send any information to external servers. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my data?

Simply uninstall Selah to remove all app data. You can also revoke calendar access in System Settings at any time.

System Requirements

  • macOS 11.0 (Big Sur) or later
  • Mac with Apple Silicon or Intel processor
  • Calendar app with configured accounts
